LearnX — Privacy & Data

Last updated: March 2, 2026

1. Privacy Policy

Who we are

LearnX is a learning cards app that delivers interesting facts across categories. This page describes how we handle your data.

Data we collect

All data is stored locally on your device only. We do not transmit or collect personal data on our servers. The app stores:

  • Progress: Last position in a deck, favorites, Easy/Hard ratings
  • Preferences: Language, theme (light/dark), onboarding and category choices

This data is used solely to provide app functionality (e.g. "Continue where you left off"). No account or sign-in is required.

Third parties

  • In-app purchases: Processed by Apple (iOS) or Google (Android). Payment data is handled by them; we do not collect or store payment details.
  • Google Fonts: The app may load fonts from Google’s servers for typography. See Google’s privacy policy.
  • External links: The app may open links (e.g. support, store). Those sites have their own privacy policies.

What we do not do

We do not use analytics, crash reporting, or advertising. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing.

Data retention

Data remains on your device until you uninstall the app. We do not retain copies elsewhere.

Your rights

You can delete all data by uninstalling the app. There is no account, so no server-held data to request or delete.

Children

LearnX is not specifically directed at children. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at andysmithfeder@gmail.com.

Changes

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date at the top will change. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

2. Data Management

How to view or delete your data

All data is stored on your device only:

  • View: Your progress and preferences are shown in the app (favorites, last position, settings).
  • Delete: Uninstalling the app removes all stored data. We do not have copies.

If you use Restore Purchases, purchase status is verified with Apple or Google. That verification does not send us personal data.

Purchases

In-app purchases are managed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). To manage or refund purchases, use your App Store or Google Play account settings.
